Support Our Divine Mission

At Shree Premdasbapa Godadiya Ashram – Ramwadi, Karjan, your generous donations help us carry forward a sacred mission rooted in faith, service, and community upliftment. With your support, we organize spiritual activities like Sundarkand Path, Hanumanji and Mahadev Pujas, and grand Kathas by revered speakers; care for cows in our Gaushala with devotion; conduct free food distribution (Sadavrat) thrice a week; and actively engage in temple and ashram development. We also encourage spiritual values in the younger generation by inviting students from various schools to participate in devotional singing and speeches, rewarding them and offering prasad.

Every Hindu festival—from Ram Navami and Janmashtami to Hanuman Jayanti, Guru Purnima, Purnima events, and Shravan Sundays—is celebrated with Annadan and Bhandara. Each rupee you offer becomes a part of this divine cycle of devotion, dharma, and seva.
